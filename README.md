# Apparel Demand Analysis & Forecasting Dashboard

## Business Problem

Retail businesses must balance inventory availability with demand uncertainty. Overstocking increases holding costs, while understocking can result in missed sales opportunities.

The objective of this project was to analyze apparel demand data to identify sales trends, understand the impact of special events on demand, and provide insights that support more effective inventory and forecasting decisions.

## Dataset

The dataset contains daily apparel sales information, including:

* Date
* Regular Unit Sales
* Race Unit Sales
* Race Event Information

The data captures both normal sales activity and demand associated with special events, allowing for a comparison of baseline demand versus event-driven demand.

## Dashboard Features

* Daily demand trend analysis
* Event-driven sales analysis
* KPI monitoring and performance tracking
* Interactive filtering and drill-down capabilities
* Forecasting and demand planning support

## Key Insights

* Apparel demand fluctuates throughout the year and is influenced by both normal purchasing behavior and event-related activity.
* Special events contribute to noticeable increases in sales volume during specific periods.
* Historical demand patterns provide valuable information for future inventory planning.
* Interactive dashboards allow decision-makers to quickly identify trends, monitor performance, and evaluate demand drivers.

## Business Value

This analysis demonstrates how data visualization and business intelligence tools can be used to convert raw operational data into meaningful insights. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by helping businesses better understand demand behavior, improve inventory planning, and anticipate future sales patterns.
